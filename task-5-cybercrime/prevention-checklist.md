# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or official organization
- Do not click on suspicious links received through SMS, email, or social media
- Always verify the identity of callers before sharing any personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions
- Never approve unknown UPI collect requests without checking details carefully
- Do not scan random QR codes, especially when you are supposed to receive money
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for any unusual activity
- Immediately report any cyber fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
- In case of financial fraud, call the Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 as soon as possible
