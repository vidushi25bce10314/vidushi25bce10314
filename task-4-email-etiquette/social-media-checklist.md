# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- Think before posting anything online
- Keep your profiles professional and respectful
- Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing it
- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages

## Don’ts
- Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or OTP
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or controversial content
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
- Avoid oversharing your daily activities or location
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
